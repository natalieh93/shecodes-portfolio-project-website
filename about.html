ABOUT ME

I was born in Vienna, Austria to a Canadian father and a Filipina mother. Growing up in a bicultural environment has helped me to not only grow as a person and improve my character, but also given me a better idea of the opportunities I might have in the future. During high school and after developing a passion for photography, I knew that I was interested in building a career out of my creative bent.

After obtaining my Communication degrees, I took some time to think about my future and career path. Thanks to the recommendation of a family friend, I decided to dive into the world of coding with SheCodes at the beginning of 2021. This milestone changed my life completely. SheCodes' well-structured workshops enabled me to learn the Front-End Development fundamentals in less than half a year. The projects that are showcased on my website were managed using Git and GitHub to help streamline the development workflow.
