ABOUT ME

Hi, I am Natalie Hood.

A Vienna-based Front-End Developer in training

I was born and raised in Vienna, Austria to a Canadian father and a Filipina mother. Growing up in a bicultural environment has helped me to not only grow as a person and improve my character, but also given me a better idea of the opportunities I might have in the future. During high school and after developing a passion for photography, I knew that I was interested in building a career out of my creative bent.

After obtaining my Communication degrees, I took some time to think about my future and career path. Thanks to the recommendation of a family friend, I decided to dive into the world of coding with SheCodes at the beginning of 2021. This milestone changed my life completely. SheCodes' well-structured workshops enabled me to learn the Front-End Development fundamentals in less than half a year. The projects that are showcased on my website were managed using Git and GitHub to help streamline the development workflow.

Because I am drawn to a dynamic career that combines my interest in learning and helping others, I am aspiring to pursue a career as a Front-End Developer. With this job, I will be able to develop beautiful, interactive websites that are responsive across all devices, and learn new techniques to expand my skill set to meet the needs of my potential clients. After SheCodes, I am planning to continue my coding journey and learn the JavaScript frameworks, programming languages, such as Python, and much more.
